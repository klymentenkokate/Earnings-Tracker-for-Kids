Earnings Tracker for Kids is a household tool where kids earn real money by doing activities and completing quests. Parents set the rules — interest rates, tax, quests — and the app keeps an honest, transparent record of everything.

🚀
Getting Started

When you first open the app, sign in or create an account. You'll be prompted to Create Your Household — give it a family name and your name in the app. You become the Admin automatically.

From there, add kids using the + Add Kid button, set their avatar, name, and color. Then invite other family members via ⚙️ Household Settings → Invite a Member.

Yes. The app syncs across devices in real time using cloud sync. Any family member with an account and a household invite can open the app and see the same data. Actions by other members appear automatically and are tracked in the activity log with the person's name.

From the home screen, click + Add Kid in the top navigation bar. You can set the kid's name, avatar emoji, and a colour theme. Each kid has their own balance, activities, quests, and log — completely separate from siblings.

Activities & Earning

Kids earn through two main ways:

  • Activities — timed tasks like reading, maths, or chores. Each activity has an hourly rate. Start the timer when the activity begins, stop it when it ends. Earnings are calculated automatically.
  • Quests — one-off or recurring tasks with a flat reward (e.g. "Score a goal — €10"). Complete a quest to earn the reward instantly.

Open a kid's dashboard and tap the ⚙️ gear icon (Kid Settings). You can add, rename, and set the hourly rate for each activity. You can also change the emoji and colour for each one. Activities are per kid, so siblings can have different sets.

Yes. On each activity card there is a manual entry option where you can type in hours and minutes directly. Manual entries are marked with a ✏️ in the log so you can tell them apart from timer sessions.

Yes. Open the 📋 Log (button in the action bar at the bottom). Each activity session has an ✏️ edit button and a ✕ delete button. Deleting a session adjusts the balance automatically. Admins can also undo any action from the audit log in Household Settings.

Quests

A quest is a task created at the household level with a flat cash reward. Unlike timed activities, quests pay a fixed amount when completed — for example "Score a goal in a match → €10" or "Do 20 push-ups → €0.50".

Every quest has a sponsor — the family member who created it. The sponsor's name is shown on the quest card and in the activity log.

  • Every time it happens — unlimited completions, no cooldown (e.g. "Score a goal")
  • Once (one-time) — can only be completed once ever
  • Once per day — resets at midnight each day
  • 2× per week — up to two completions per calendar week
  • Once per week — one completion per calendar week
  • Once per month — one completion per calendar month

When the limit is reached, the Complete button greys out until the next period.

Admins create quests and they go live immediately.

Editors and Champions can suggest quests, but these go into a ⏳ Pending queue. An admin must approve, edit, or reject them before kids can see and complete the quest. Admins see the pending queue at the top of the Quests tab.

Yes. When creating a quest with multiple kids in the household, you can assign it to All Kids or select specific kids. Each kid completes quests independently — one sibling finishing a quest doesn't affect another's access to it.

Balance & Rewards

Tap 💸 Redeem in the action bar. Enter the amount and what it's being spent on (e.g. "New toy", "Savings"). The balance is reduced immediately and the redemption appears in the activity log. A parent then gives the actual cash.

Go to the 📈 Charts tab on a kid's dashboard and scroll to Earnings Sources. It shows a breakdown of:

  • Activities — total from timed tasks
  • Quests — total from quests, with each sponsor listed and their individual quests and amounts
  • Interest & Bonuses — monthly interest and surprise bonuses

The Surprise Chest is a bonus system that occasionally rewards kids with extras like a cash bonus or a double-earnings multiplier on their next session. It's designed to keep things exciting and unpredictable — kids never know exactly when or what they'll get.

🏛️
Interest & Tax

Interest is a percentage added to a kid's balance each month — a reward for saving. Set the rate in ⚙️ Kid Settings → Monthly Interest %. The default is 1%. Apply it manually with 📈 Apply Interest, or it applies automatically at the start of a new month.

Household tax is a percentage deducted from a kid's balance each month — their share of keeping the household running (rent, food, bills). Set the rate in ⚙️ Household Settings → Household Tax. Hovering over the 🏛️ badge on a kid's dashboard explains this to them in kid-friendly language.

Tax is applied manually with 🏛️ Apply Tax or automatically at month rollover. It appears in the log and in the gross vs. net breakdown.

📋
Activity Log

Tap the 📋 Log button in the action bar at the bottom of any kid's dashboard. The log modal shows all entries for that kid with filters by type: Activities, Quests, Redeemed, Interest, and Tax. If there are multiple kids in the household, you can switch between kids using the kid filter at the top.

Yes. Admins can undo any action from the Activity Log section inside ⚙️ Household Settings. Each entry has an Undo button that restores the previous state for the whole household — including balance adjustments. The log also shows which family member performed each action.

From the 🏠 Home screen, go to the 📋 Log tab. This shows a combined log of all kids in the household, with colour-coded kid chips on each entry. You can filter by type including a dedicated ⚔️ Quests filter.

📈
Charts & Projections

The 📈 Charts tab on a kid's dashboard has four views:

  • Breakdown — a pie chart of earnings by activity type
  • Daily — a bar chart of earnings per day
  • Monthly — earnings per month
  • Yearly — earnings per year

Below the chart, Revenue Projections estimate end-of-month and end-of-year balances based on recent activity. And the Earnings Sources section breaks down earnings into Activities, Quests (with per-sponsor detail), and Interest.

Data & Sync

Yes. Every action saves locally to your browser immediately. If you are signed in and connected to the internet, changes also sync to the cloud automatically so all family members on any device see the latest data.

Use the 💾 Save Backup button in the action bar to download a JSON file of a kid's data. Use 📂 Load Backup to restore it. Backups are per kid, not the whole household. Cloud sync is the recommended way to keep data safe across devices.

If you are signed in with cloud sync enabled, your data is safe — just sign in again and it will reload. If you were using the app in local-only mode without an account, clearing browser data will erase everything. We strongly recommend creating an account to protect your data.
